# Adidas Sales Performance Dashboard (Tableau)
## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing Adidas sales performance across products, regions, retailers, and sales methods.
The goal? To transform raw sales data into clear, actionable insights that can support strategic business decisions in retail and e-commerce.
## Objectives
- Analyze sales performance trends over time
- Identify top-performing products and regions
- Evaluate sales channels effectiveness
- Provide a holistic business snapshot using KPIs
## Tools Used
	•	Tableau (Data Visualization)
	•	Excel (Data Preparation & Cleaning)
## Key Insights
- Product Performance
  - Men’s Street Footwear emerged as the top-performing category:
	- Highest average operating margin: 44.6%
	- Highest units sold: 593,320
- Regional Performance
  - The West region led in profitability:
	- Operating Profit: $89.61M
- Retailer Performance
  - West Gear generated the highest total sales:
	- $242.96M
- Sales Methods Analysis
  - Online channel recorded the highest units sold:
	- 939,093 units
	- Outlet channel generated the highest total sales:
	- $356.64M
- Time Trends
  - Peak sales occurred in July 2021:
	- $78.33M in total sales
- Geographic Insights
  - New York recorded the highest sales by state:
	- $64.23M
## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Sales: $899,902,125
- Total Operating Profit: $332,134,761
- Total Units Sold: 2,478,861
- Average Price per Unit: $45.22
- Number of Product Categories: 6
- Retailer Count: 6
## Dashboard Features
	- Interactive filters for deeper analysis
	- Multiple visualizations:
	- Bar charts
	- Column charts
	- Pie charts
	- Area chart (time series)
	- Treemap (geographical distribution)
	- Clean KPI summary for quick decision-making
## Business Recommendations
- Invest more in Men’s Street Footwear due to strong margins and demand
- Strengthen online sales strategy to leverage high volume
- Optimize outlet channel pricing to sustain revenue leadership
- Focus expansion efforts on high-performing regions like the West

This dashboard demonstrates the ability to:

	- Communicate findings clearly for stakeholders
